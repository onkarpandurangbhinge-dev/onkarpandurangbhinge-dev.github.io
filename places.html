Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva, the temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake. Thousands of devotees visit the temple every day.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra, located about 70 km from Solapur. It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple. Millions of devotees visit during Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi.

Akkalkot

Akkalkot is an important spiritual destination located 40 km from Solapur. It is famous for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj.

Solapur Fort

Solapur Fort, also known as Bhuikot Fort, is a historic monument built during the Bahmani Sultanate period.
