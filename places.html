Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva, the temple is located near the peaceful Siddheshwar Lake in the center of the city. Thousands of devotees visit the temple every day and the temple becomes especially crowded during the Siddheshwar Yatra festival.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra and is located about 70 km from Solapur. It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple dedicated to Lord Vitthal. The town becomes a major spiritual center during the Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi festivals when millions of devotees participate in the Wari pilgrimage.

Tuljapur

Tuljapur is a famous pilgrimage destination located near Solapur and is home to the renowned Tulja Bhavani Temple. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Tulja Bhavani and is one of the important Shakti Peethas in Maharashtra. Thousands of devotees visit this temple throughout the year, especially during Navratri.
