How to Reach Solapur

By Road

Solapur is connected by highways to Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Bengaluru.

  • Pune – 250 km
  • Hyderabad – 300 km
  • Mumbai – 400 km
  • Bengaluru – 380 km

By Rail

Solapur Railway Station is a major junction connecting Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad and Chennai.

By Air

The nearest major airports are Pune International Airport and Hyderabad International Airport.

Local Transport

  • Auto rickshaws
  • Taxis
  • City buses
